  • What is Welfare Service Center?

    • It is a private social welfare delivery system that was first developed in Korea, based on Welfare Service Law.
    • It conducts variety of services such as welfare service, case management, organizing local community and the rest.
    • There are 464 centers through out the nation, especially 98 centers operating in Seoul.

Characteristics of Main Visitors

    • Service available to residents in Permanent rental apartment
    • Wolgye Jugong district 1, Wolgye 2-dong, Nowon-gu
    • Main visitors
    • Children, Teenagers, Adults, Elders (All generations)
    • Financial state of visitors
    • Recipients of basic living aid, Low income class, Secondary income class, Ordinary citizens
    • Attribute of the visitors
    • Divorced families, Single-parent families, Grandparent-grandchild families, Alcoholics, Domestic violence victims, Elders living alone, The handicapped (mentally or physically challenged)
    • Approximately 1,200 people benefit from welfare services daily, using 76 services in 4 major departments
      • about 300 Children & Teens - Single-parent families(36%), Grandparent-grandchild families(18%), Disabled parent families(10%)
      • about 200 Adults - Volunteers, Service program users, Foreign immigrant women, Resident cafe users
      • about 670 Elders - Elders living alone(40%), Elder couples(12%)
        [over eighties(40%), seventies(47%), sixties(13%)]
